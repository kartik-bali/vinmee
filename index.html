About Us

Vinmee International


We introduce ourselves as one of the dynamic enterprises engaged in the manufacture and supplier of gorgeous and warm collection of knitted fabrics and garments for ladies, men and kids. Providing unmatched quality products, that signifies reliability and trust, we offer our customers with a range that has high fashion appeal and comfort.


The company has redefined the rules of trade by implementing advanced technology with ethical business principles, and boasts of the ability to meet large orders with precision and timely delivery. We strive to traverse customer requirements and consolidate ourselves on the ground of quality and world class service.
